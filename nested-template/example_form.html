Simple searches

Here we search for solr documents, regardless of whether they are corresponding to a Digital Library Object (DLO) or a descriptor of such an object.

  • Find Albert Einstein, i.e., find the descriptor of a letter authored by Albert. agent_name:albert (1 hit)
  • Find descriptors of homiliae-super-psalmos describing:manus-vmanus-2011-dec-ha-object71279 (11 hits)
  • Select nodes that are descriptors (describing other nodes) described:false (205 hits)
  • Select nodes that are DLOs (i.e., described by other nodes) described:true (15 hits)
  • Both first and last name of a name descriptor agent_name:(Waage Sandø) (1 hits)
  • note:"Waage Sandø" in a note (which belongs to a DLO, i.e., a described node). This search include a field list, *,[child], such that all child nodes (descriptors) appear as well. (1 hits)
  • Search for instance of Georg Brandes where he is in the recieving end of a correspondence agent_name:(georg brandes) AND entity_type:rcp (8 hits)
  • Search for instance of Georg Brandes where he is the author of a message agent_name:(georg brandes) AND entity_type:aut (1 hits)
  • The should ideally only be one manuscript having shelf_mark_verbatim:"Cod. Sim. Heb. 35" (1 hits) That assertion turned out to be correct, at least in this corpus.
